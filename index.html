OUR ROOTS

OpenGrowth.org is a Non-profit 501(c)(3) organization based in Palo Alto, California. The primary purpose of OpenGrowth.org is to encourage, incubate and support the development of new social impact organizations that can help solve some of society's most complex problems. OpenGrowth.org carefully vets exceptional social entrepreneurs and teams and then helps them incubate and launch high impact charitable organizations and initiatives. OpenGrowth.org specifically provides training and education to social entrepreneurs and teams, while also providing assistance with financial support and grants to achieve the maximum societal impact at scale. Tax ID Number: EIN # 83-3024808

OUR VISION

We believe our social innovation initiatives (working in partnership with social entrepreneurs and businesses) will genuinely bring open opportunities and inclusiveness to all – and help make the world a better place. We strive to help our partner organizations (non-profit social impact organizations, philanthropists, social entrepreneurs, and corporations) achieve their social responsibility goals and objectives thereby helping us tackle some of society's most complex problems.

Our Focus

We want to be the "Hub" for driving "DO GOOD" - Hub of content, global partners, change makers, entrepreneurial teams, changemakers, software developers, AI/ML/Data Science experts & Media Professionals - who want to make the world a better place.

Do Well, Do Good

There is a tremendous market need for OpenGrowth.org to become a trusted "Silicon Valley" innovation partner for our organizations and social entrepreneurs, thereby helping each of our partner organizations and social entrepreneurs to realize their unique social impact mission, thereby making the world a better place.
